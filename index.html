Karim El-Sharkawy

Email: kelshark@purdue.edu

Welcome to my corner of the internet! I'm an undergraduate at the Department of Mathematics at Purdue University, with a keen interest in applied mathematics and environmental data science. My passion lies at both seperate studies and their intersection, fueled by a deep commitment to sustainability. My ultimate goal is to use applied/numerical linear algebra along with machine learning to address problems within maths and atmospheric studies.

My research and projects are aimed at maturing my mathematical abilities and achieving this goal. From developing innovative mathematical models to collaborating on interdisciplinary projects, I am dedicated to advancing knowledge and innovation in pursuit of a greener future.

But life isn't just about work, right? Beyond academia, I enjoy learning new languages, reading poetry, and listening to music. Have a wonderful day!
